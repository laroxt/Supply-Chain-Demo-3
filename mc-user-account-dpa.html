A. GOALS

The aim of the Supply Chain System is to gather information for National supply chain development. Government of Malaysia shall be analyzed the obtained data from the system in order to improve current local industry ecosystem and used for future Procurement/Project Reference, this will help lessen the needs for foreign expertise in the future and to use the Procurement/Project as a benchmark in terms of financial figure for the next Government’s Procurement/Project.
